Turn-Based-Combat-Platformer or TBCP

Disclaimer: The playable Game is very much an older build!

Explore your way trough this test level with the help of some powerups
Controls: WASD to move, Space to jump, F to interact, Left mouse click hit enemies, Right mouse click to grapple (you can grapple on white surfaces), left shift to dash, hold Z to super dash. In combat select a skill to use, then click on an enemy to target it, finally press 'Space' to unleash your skill
